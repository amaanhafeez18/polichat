1. Q: What is the vision statement of PSW?
   A: Using technology and innovative solutions to enable ease in achieving effective compliance with national regulations, especially for cross-border trade and related services.

2. Q: What is the mission statement of PSW?
   A: Establish a single electronic platform to efficiently meet requirements for the regulation of imports, exports, and transit trade while supporting the simplification, harmonization, and digitization of related processes to improve ease of doing business and compliance.

3. Q: What does PSW strive for in its values?
   A: PSW commits to constant effort and innovation in its drive towards excellence.

   A: PSW challenges every established process, procedure, document, policy, and law while avoiding assumptions and disregarding the status quo for bringing improvements.

6. Q: What does PSW believe about learning?

7. Q: How does PSW promote inclusivity?

10. Q: What is one of the main objectives of PSW?
    A: To reduce time, cost, and complexity while improving the quality of experience for all stakeholders to ensure ease of doing business in cross-border trade.

11. Q: What role does PSW aim to play according to the PSW Act 2021?

15. Q: How does PSW support other entities?
